**HCS Monk Brewmaster Script v1.0.0**

A complete Brewmaster tank rotation for Project Sylvanas with IZI SDK support. Dual logic, smart stagger, burst sync, emergency healing, and more.

**Features:**

✅ **Dual Logic** — Toggle between High Key (Survival) and Low Key (Max DPS)
✅ **Smart Stagger** — Auto-Purify on heavy stagger; use Purifying Brew before capping charges
✅ **Auto Survival** — Fortifying / Celestial / Dampen Harm on dynamic HP thresholds (Survival mode)
✅ **Burst Sync** — Stack Bonedust Brew → Weapons of Order → Niuzao when both WoO and Niuzao ready
✅ **Energy Anti-Cap** — Expel Harm when energy above threshold to avoid wasting
✅ **Auto-Execute** — Touch of Death priority on low-HP targets (Smart targeting)
✅ **Smart AoE** — SCK priority when 5+ enemies (swap rotation)
✅ **Emergency Healing** — Vivify + Expel Harm when below critical HP%
✅ **Shado-Pan & Master of Harmony** builds
✅ **M+ S3 tank buster** reactions
✅ **Self-healing** — Expel Harm, potion, Fortifying, Celestial (sliders)
✅ **Interrupts/stuns** with boss checks (no wasted GCDs)
✅ **Targeting:** Manual / Casters first / Skull first / Smart
✅ **Tiger's Lust** root dispel
✅ **Right-click attack** (Chi Burst range pull)
✅ **Full cooldown** management

**Note:** Stuns and pushback skip bosses to avoid wasting GCDs. Self-healing works with no target. Logic mode sliders: Emergency HP%, Vivify HP%, Fortifying HP%, Celestial HP%, Dampen Harm HP%, Energy anti-cap %.
